Participant Support

Support and Well-being Resources

This resource sheet is provided to all research participants regardless of current wellbeing. You do not need to be in crisis to use any of these services. Please keep the downloaded PDF in a safe place.

If You Need Urgent Help Right Now

  • Call 999 if you or someone else is in immediate danger.
  • Visit your nearest A&E department for urgent medical help.
  • Call NHS 111 and select option 2 for the NHS urgent mental health line.
  • Text SHOUT to 85258 for 24/7 crisis text support.
  • Call Samaritans on 116 123 for 24/7 confidential listening.
