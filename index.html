AI-Supported Belonging

Belonging Across Places

Using AI to support East Asian migrants in the UK.

This project asks how conversational AI and digital technologies might support human connection rather than replace it, especially for first-generation East Asian migrants living in the UK.

The doctoral programme, AI as Relational Infrastructure: Culturally Attuned Support for Multi-Sited Belonging, examines how AI systems can become more culturally attuned, relationally careful, and accountable to lived migration experiences.

The work brings together human-computer interaction, design research, culturally responsive AI, and relational wellbeing.
